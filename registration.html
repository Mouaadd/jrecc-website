Registration

You can register to the winter school !

Access to the Winter School is subject to payment of a fee which enables us to partially cover the organization costs.


Registration rate Before 2024/01/31 2024/02/04 After 2024/01/31 2024/02/04
Students/Phd Student/Fresh Graduates Currently Unemployed 500 MAD 1000 MAD
Teachers/Researchers 1000 MAD 2000 MAD
Firms/Others 2000 MAD 4000 MAD

If you are interested, please fill that form . We will get back to you soon

As pre-payment of the fee is a condition for participation. Please send us an evidence of payment at ecc_data@centrale-casablanca.ma!

Please note that personnel and students from OCP, UM6P, and Ecole Centrale Casablanca are exempt from the participation fee.

Once your registration confirmed by your badge reception, it authorizes you to participate in the conferences, the posters sessions and the round tables.

However, your transportation, meals and accommodation are entirely at your expense.

Good news! At the request of many of you, limited spots for rooms on the UM6P campus are currently available. Additionally, the registration deadline for the first rates has been extended from January 31, 2024, to February 4, 2024 - this is the final deadline before the registration fee double
Ensure you complete your registration and payment before February 4, 2024, indicating the duration of your stay. For those who have already filled the registration form before the 2024/01/20, you can provide additional accomodaton information using this form. The rate is 250 Dhs per night, including breakfast. It is crucial to note that the only accepted mode of payment on-site is by credit card. However, at the canteen, purchasing tickets with cash is possible. Additionally, it's worth mentioning that there are several restaurants on the campus

Furthermore, we want to emphasize that after February 4, 2024, only registrations will be accepted, and accommodation requests will not be accommodated..

You can contact that email adress for more informations: ecc_data@centrale-casablanca.ma
