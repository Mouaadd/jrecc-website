Objective

Our winter data science school focuses on Generative AI. Our main goals are to provide a deep understanding of generative AI techniques, foster collaboration and networking, promote practical skills through conferences, encourage innovation through a hackathon, present research projects, and recognize excellence through awards. We also aim to facilitate interdisciplinary learning by involving experts from various fields. Overall, our goal is to enable participants to expand their knowledge, interact with experts, and develop practical skills in the field of generative AI.

Join us in exploring the world of Generative AI. For more details, refer to our program flyer, and stay updated by following us on LinkedIn.

During this exciting week, you can expect:

  • Keynote Presentations
  • Panel Discussions
  • Business Case Studies
  • Research Posters Presentations
  • Hackathon Competition & Awards

Audience

This event is tailored for:

  • PhD and Master's students
  • Experienced engineers and researchers
  • Entrepreneurs and innovators interested in data sciences

Experienced professionals in data science and generative AI are also warmly welcomed.

Venue

The winter school will take place on Monday, February 26, 2024, and Tuesday, February 27, 2024, in the main room of the Conference Center at EMINES, UM6P. The conferences will focus on generative AI.
On Friday, March 1, 2024, it will be held at École Centrale Casablanca in amphitheater 1 and/or 2 (depending on attendance), and the conferences will cover advances in AI.

Here are the locations:
  • EMINES, UM6P: EMINES - Université Mohammed VI Polytechnique - LOT 660- HAY MOULAY RACHID - 43150 BEN GUERIR
  • École Centrale Casablanca: Ville Verte Côté Latéral Est à la forêt Boukoura
