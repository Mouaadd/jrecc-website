Organization

The Winter Data Science School on "Generative AI" is organized by Centrale Casablanca: Teaching and Research Unit "Applied Mathematics Computer and D@ta Sciences" (UTER MID@S), in collaboration with Emines-UM6P: Data Science and Industrial Process, Chair - OCP S.A., and Ecole Polytechnique - France (l'X), Centre de Mathématiques Appliquées (CMPAX).
