Schedule (provisional)

During the initial two days (26 and 27 February), the program will be hosted at UM6P, featuring sessions elucidating introductory insights into Generative AI by esteemed speakers like Eric Moulines and Eric Xing.

Following these instructive days, the subsequent 48 hours (28 and 29 February) will be dedicated to an immersive Hackathon, where students will engage in practical application development under the guidance of global mentors, as detailed in the provided schedule.

The event concludes on the final day (01 March) at Ecole Centrale Casablanca, with a comprehensive wrap-up session, encompassing notable presentations and outcomes from the hackathon, offering a comprehensive culmination to the event.
